My Learning Journey

My journey in tech started with a curiosity about how things work. I've always wondered about the inner workings of apps and how applications are created. Coming from a very small country where we barely had access to computers, we only got a chance to use one once a week. This limited access fueled my curiosity even more, and I often found myself dreaming of becoming a software engineer. I aspire to bring technological advancements to my country and make a meaningful impact through software engineering.
